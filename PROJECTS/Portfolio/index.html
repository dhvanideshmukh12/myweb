The Importance of Web Fonts

Fonts do more than display text — they communicate tone, style, and intent. Choosing the right font can make content more engaging, easier to read, and more aligned with your brand.

Sans-serif fonts like Helvetica and Arial offer a modern, clean appearance and are great for digital screens. On the other hand, serif fonts like Times New Roman or Georgia are often used for more formal or traditional contexts.

In responsive design, it’s important to use scalable units and media queries to ensure text remains legible across all devices. Services like Google Fonts make it easy to include custom typefaces without compromising performance.
